### About  
**synavera_discorporated** is an independent software development and research entity dedicated to building systems that explain themselves.  
Every project released under the Synavera banner follows one unbreakable rule: **clarity is security**.  

We design tools, frameworks, and protocols that are transparent by nature — auditable, modular, and answerable to their users.  
Our philosophy is simple: technology should respect its operator, expose its reasoning, and leave an immutable trail of accountability.

---

### Principles  
Each Synavera project adheres to the **Synavera Script Etiquette (SSE)**, a covenant of structure, ethics, and clarity.

- **Clarity as Security** — Code must be explicit, legible, and free of hidden logic.  
- **Predictable and Auditable Faults** — Systems must never fail silently.  
- **Modularity and Interface Integrity** — Components must be composable and replaceable.  
- **Documentation as Narrative Accountability** — Every file tells the story of its intent.  
- **Deliberate Aesthetics** — Visual order reflects disciplined thought.  
- **Ethical Transparency and User Agency** — Consent is explicit; control is granular.  
- **Immutable Audit Trails** — History is recorded, verifiable, and tamper-evident.
